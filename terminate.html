Account Deletion

You can delete your free @open.email account at any time directly from the app.

  • Open the app on your device
  • Sign in with your account
  • Navigate to Settings > Account > Delete Account

Account deletion is immediate and permanent. All messages and associated data will be erased, and your email address will be released for others to use.

Inactive Accounts

If you do not sign in for 90 consecutive days, your free account will be automatically terminated. This includes the permanent deletion of all associated data, and your @open.email address will become available for registration by others.

If you no longer use your account, you don't need to take any action, it will expire automatically after this period of inactivity.

For any questions, please contact us at our OpenEmail address support@open.email.
